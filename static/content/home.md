# Welcome to Listening to Indonesia

**Listening to Indonesia** is a high-frequency monitoring initiative that tracks the welfare and economic conditions of Indonesian households through regular phone surveys.

## About the Survey

The survey collects data from **3,600 households** across Indonesia using Computer-Assisted Telephone Interviews (CATI). This approach allows for rapid data collection and timely insights into how households are coping with economic changes.

## Key Features

- **Monthly data collection** for up-to-date insights
- **Nationally representative** sample design
- **Multiple welfare dimensions** including income, food security, and public services
- **Rapid turnaround** from data collection to analysis
