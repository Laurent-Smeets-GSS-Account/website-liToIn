## Data Available

The food security module uses the internationally comparable FIES methodology:

1. Worried about not having enough food
2. Unable to eat healthy and nutritious food
3. Ate only a few kinds of foods
4. Skipped meals
5. Ate less than should
6. Ran out of food
7. Hungry but did not eat
8. Went without eating for a whole day
